SaladCo Jell-O Salad Recipe

1 cafe chicken salad
1 pack lemon jell-o
water
bundt cake mold

Instructions:
Refrigerate chicken salad for at least 12 hours beforehand.
Boil 1 cup water on the stove.
Pour jell-o into a mixing bowl, add boiling water until jell-o dissolves
Add 1 cup cold water
Pour mixture in bundt cake mold
Add refrigerated salad to mold
Stir until approximately evenly distributed
Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until gelatin is firm.
Serve.
